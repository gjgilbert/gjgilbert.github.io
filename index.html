Hi, I'm Greg Gilbert!

I'm a postdoctoral scholar in the Astronomy department at Caltech

My research primarily focuses on understanding the architectures, demographics, and dynamics of exoplanetary systems. I'm also broadly interested in statistical methods and science pedagogy.

I'm currently working with the Keck Planet Finder team, developing software to faciliate high-resolution spectroscopy, in particular Extreme Precision Radial Velocity (EPRV) measurements of exoplanets
