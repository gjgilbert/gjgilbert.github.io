Multiplanet System Architectures

My research is broadly on trying to understand the orbital architectures of exoplanetary systems with multiple planets.

The arrangements of masses, orbital periods, and mutual inclinations within each planetary system are markedly nonrandom, but describing these patterns is an inherently complex task. I'm leveraging ideas from information theory in order to capture the global architecture of each system, with an eye towards classifying subpopulations of systems and identifying which systems are most likely to host additional undetected planets.
